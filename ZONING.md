# Zoning Model

- Clean Zone: electronics, CAD, RF bench, documentation.
- Fabrication Zone: cabinetry, woodworking, assembly.
- Dirty Zone: cutting, sanding, material prep.
- Fume / Composite Zone: soldering, epoxy, fiberglass.
- Storage Zone: sheet goods, long stock, small parts.

Zones are adjacent but intentionally ordered to manage contamination and airflow.
