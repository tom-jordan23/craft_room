# Infrastructure Plan

## Electrical
- Multiple dedicated circuits for benches and stationary tools.
- Bench-height receptacles and overhead drops where appropriate.

## Lighting
- Ambient lighting for general work.
- Task lighting at benches and assembly areas.
- Color-accurate lighting for finishing and inspection.

## Ventilation
- Repositionable local exhaust hood vented outdoors.
- Make-up air considerations to avoid negative pressure issues.
